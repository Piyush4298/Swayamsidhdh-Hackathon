Dr.Suresh R. Parekar

Prof. & Academic Coordinator (Institute level), Civil Engineering Department, AISSMS COE

Hello everyone, I Dr. Suresh R Parekar would like to express my views on UNNAT BHARAT ABHYAN a social technical hackathon organised by AISSMS COE. To begin with I would like to say a few words on one of the basic need of a man i.e. Food which is affected directly by farming. Now a days we are all aware that the food we get is not that healthy or organic in sofesticated words. So what can be done? Asking this question would be very outdated, as we all know that this problem has got an excellent solution which we call Organic Farming but again many of the farmer's are still unaware of this healthy farming. The another issue that takes a stand is water management and energy resources. Many of the farmers and villages are still deprived of these facilities while many villages are emerging. Because of lack of basic amenities and infrastructure, the education and training are hampered to some extent. Artisan, Industries and livelihood are developing at a greater pace. While considering all this, we somewhere have to start encouraging women and work on Women empowerment. There are many such things and points to be concerned for the developing India. AISSMS COE is giving you an opportunity to beat your brains out and think on the above topics and give a convenient and economic solution. We invite you to take part in this and suggest your ideas. Welcome one welcome all..!!
